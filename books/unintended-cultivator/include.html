NOTE This series is being written on Royal Road; I have read chapters there that have not yet been published, up to volume 9.

Review

Like Dragon Heart, this series focuses more on the personal impact and toll of cultivation and slaughter. This series has a nice slice-of-life style to it, but it sufficiently paced to not be tedious. With instances of politicing, noble house and sect thrashing and building, this is an exciting read.

Progression

  • Qi-condensing — gather Qi in dantian and expand meridians.
  • Foundation formation
  • Core formation — construct core.
  • Nascent soul — awakening of ones soul.
Once at peak nascent soul, the Heaven's forces a cultivator to ascend. The nature of ascension is not known; most believe it is a rise to godhood, but others such as Sen believe that they will rise to a greater plane, and start the entire journey over as they become the weakest in the plane.
