Review

Book series is in progress.

Magic System

Most people are born with a core (5/6). A core can hold essence, and later Qi, Chakra and Reiki. Early on in advancement ther burn channels throughout the body to channel essence, and form pattern around their core for cycling and purification of essence.

In the Trial of beggining one faces trials for the 8 core paths. Often one will be called to a certain path or multiple. Each artist takes on the characters of the path and create techniques around its element.

  • Path of Earth - slow but strong.
  • Path of Water - nimble and flowing.
  • Path of Fire - ferocious and wild.
  • Path of Air - quick.
  • Path of Lightness
  • Path of Darkness - sneaky and silent.
  • Path of Weakness - sleaky and slimy.
  • Path of Power - blunt and strong.

Beats are animals with cores. Cores may be removed from beasts and cycled, though the power gained is much less than the level of the core.

There are four different types of energy, each of which is unlocked at a certain belt. The belt before this they can sense and make the new energy type, but not use it without being destroyed (potentially):

  • Essence - a gaseous-like substance.
  • Qi - a watery-like substance. 1/25 as powerul as Essence.
  • Chakra - a syrupy-like substance. 1/100 as powerful as Essence.
  • Reiki - an almost-solid substance. 1/1000 as powerful as Essence.
  • Soulcore - ??. 1/?? as powerful as Essence.

Techniques

All martial artists will train in techniques over the course of their Path. There are nine overall techniques, which can be broken into 2 categories, and each of which can be improved 5 times in potency and cost.

Body Techniques - control of the physical self.

  • Physical - affects the body directly, strengthening blows or lending one special properties. The user must make physical contact for the technique to work.
  • Movement - affects how the body moves, increasing speed, flexibility, etc.
  • Armorer - affects how the body stands up to damage. The more damage taken, the more energy used.
  • Full-body - a combination of the previous 3 techniques, multiplying their effect many times.

Spirit Techniques - control of energy.

  • Projected - projects energy outward as an attack. Mostly used as ranged attacks but can be used to enhance physical techniques. Requires a lot of concentration.
  • Terrain - controls energy outside the body to affect the terrain using theit will.
  • Containment - used to capture someone or contain attacks. Needs extreme control and is slow to form.
  • Full-area - a combination of the previous 3 techniques, multiplying their effect many times.

Conqueror - a combination of full-body and full-area techniques, lends full control over body and spirit.

Progression

As the book is centred on the martial arts, ranking is done through belts. Belts the,selves are manifestations of the core. Cores can be veriled to hide ones presence to the spirit sense, or their core restricted to a lower belt, but otherwise a belt cannot be faked. To advance a Dan one must push the boundaries of ones core with the respective energy type. Once the maximum Dans have been reached, a Torii gate will appear. To advance to the next belt, one must enter and complete a test or which only 3 attempts are given.

Titles

Various titles exist which mark out martial artists of certain abilities, beyond the rank of their belt.

  • Master - mastered all 9 techniques in Essence. Achieved by 1/100,000 artists.
  • Grandmaster - mastered all 9 techniques in Qi. Achieved by 1/1,000,000 artists.
  • Supreme - mastered all 9 techniques in Chakra. Achieved by 1/40,000,000 artists.
  • Sage - Gold-belts or above who have touched on the true meaning of their Path and have been deemed worthy by their Path's Spirit. Achieved by 1/1,000,000,000 artists.
  • Sovereign - mastered all 9 techniques of Reiki. Achieved by 1/100,000,000 artists.
  • Scion - a Supreme who was also a Sage upon advancing to Black belt. Extremely rare. Most artists leave the world when this is achieved in search of greater things as Buryoku seems so petty and inferior to them.
  • Eternal - mastered all 9 techniques of Soulcore.

Characters

  • Herald Leroy (Roy) - main character. Introduced as a coreless freak who was kicked about in the Shah clan, he was left to die in their local dungeon when the dungeon core merged with him. He was initially out for revenge on the Shah clan and planned to kill them, but when the time came he only destroyed their leadership. He is on the Path of Power, branching to the Path of the Titanic Demon. His Ideal is to help free the oppressed and gain the power to do so. He is the son of Duncan, head of the Herald clan, and his wife, a powerful Beast. He grew rapidly as a child, bacoming powerful and crazed for bloodlust, so he was stripped of his core by his parents. He has an Oni spirit manifested in him. Becomes a fledgling Sage at Red-belt.
  • Geon - dungeon core which was striped of his power. Ended up mergin with a coreless Roy. Initially wants revengs on the Beast King and the Supreme/Sovereign of Clying Darkness as they were the ones to strip him of his power.
  • Azure - a mysterious extremely powerful being, he broke into Roy's Trials of Beginning and gave him a scroll on cycling techniques which changed at each belt. He visited a few times since then, giving him vague advice. It turns out he has also visited Hermit.
  • Tonde Aika - main character on the Path of Light.
  • Tonde Kaeru - supreme of the Tonde clan, he beats the Beast King and learns that there was an outside presence who triggered the war so he left to discover it. Discovers that Tonde Aika is in Light City, where his clan previously thought she was dead. Becomes friends and enters a relationship with Itachi Violet. Manages to become a Sage, a great achievement.
  • Hermit/Scion of Honour - previously the Sage of Ruin, he turned his back on that life. Introduced wondering the woods whilst Roy was mentally blocked from his power, he seemed disinterested and wanted to be alone. However, Roy persuaded him to help unblock his mental wall. He ended up aiding Roy and Aika whilst being attacked and tortured in the woods by members of the Itachi clan, which it turns out Hermit used to be the Head of. After fetching him a fruit which cleared his soul of corruption and allowing him to advance, he took them on as students. He became a Scion and worked alongside Duncan in the battle against the Bringer of Fire.
  • Itachi Violet - a young prodigy of the Itachi clan, a purple belt at 16. Escaped Light City with Tonde Kaeru to help protect Hermit, who was being hunted by his sister's men.
  • Herald Duncan - father of Roy, he initially believed his son to have died so was overjoyed when he discovered Roy was living. Head of the Herald clan, he advanced alongside Hermit to Scion to uniy the Seven Great Clans and lead the war against the Bringer of Fire.
