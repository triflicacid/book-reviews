Progression

Progression is split into ranks, with each rank progressively harder to reach. One levels up by levelling up all their abilities. This is commonly done through exercising them (fighting similar-rank monsters) or consuming monster cores (but this method hhits a wall at gold).

  • No-ranker - has no essences/incomplete essences.
  • Iron
  • Bronze
  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Diamond - very few in number, considered legendary.
